02Curriculum & Learning Outcomes

Four modules — from setting the stage to telling the story at scale.

Select a module to explore its focus and the outcomes participants walked away with.

Module 01 — Setting the Stage & Quick Wins

The South Canadian Rockies Experience

A dual-purpose session that introduces the Tourism Ambassador Program and immediately equips owners with practical data and marketing tools.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the program vision and the “Ambassador” role within the regional tourism economy.
  • Learn simple methods to calculate ROI using guest origin data — postal codes, zip codes, and origin surveys.
  • Identify quick wins in digital presence: reviews, online listings, and Google Business Profile optimization that drive visitation.
Module 02 — Turning Ideas into Revenue

Developing Market-Ready Products & Experiences

Building on each owner’s “starter concept,” this session focuses on packaging, pricing, and collaboration with neighbouring businesses.

Learning Outcomes

  • Transform a rough concept into a fully bookable, market-ready product.
  • Understand pricing strategies and bundling for sustainable tourism margins.
  • Learn how to collaborate with other local businesses to increase total guest value.
Module 03 — Reducing Friction

Driving Online Bookings & Conversions

Once a product exists, this module covers how to actually sell it — through the website, the booking journey, and email.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and fix barriers to booking on their own websites.
  • Learn to select and work effectively with online booking software.
  • Use email marketing to convert past guests into repeat visitors.
  • Understand the digital customer journey from dreaming to booking.
Module 04 — Storytelling at Scale

Creating Compelling Social Media

Content creation, mobile video, and using AI as an assistant to build a sustainable rhythm that doesn’t burn operators out.

Learning Outcomes

  • Master simple mobile video editing for Instagram Reels and TikTok.
  • Use AI tools to generate captions, ideas, and content instantly.
  • Create a sustainable content calendar that fits a small operator’s reality.
